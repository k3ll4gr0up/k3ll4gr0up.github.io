- Lunar Lander -

Lunar Lander is a space game written in C for my school.

The main goal of Lunar Lander is to land a spaceship on the moon. But wait. It's not graphical at all. in fact, it's a console game, which prints out a table. In there you can get the current values of height, speed and fuel. You have to decide how much fuel you burn every ten seconds.

Lunar Lander was originally written by a stundent in 1967. It was one of the first ever computer games in existance.
